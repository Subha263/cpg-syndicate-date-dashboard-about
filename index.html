Project Overview

This dashboard analyzes the US Cookies & Biscuits category covering 4 brands (Oreo, Chips Ahoy, Keebler, Private Label) across 10 retailers and 5 channels for January 2022 — December 2023. Built using NielsenIQ-style syndicated data methodology to demonstrate CPG category management analytics comparable to IRI/Circana reporting standards.

Key Terminology — 22 CPG Industry Terms Used in This Dashboard

ACV

All Commodity Volume — % of stores carrying a product weighted by each store's total sales volume

Numeric Distribution

% of stores physically stocking the product on shelf — unweighted store count measure

TDP

Total Distribution Points — ACV × Numeric Distribution — measures overall shelf presence intensity

Velocity

$ per ACV — sales efficiency per point of distribution — measures how hard each shelf placement works

FSI

Fair Share Index — Brand $ Share ÷ ACV Distribution — above category avg means overperforming vs distribution

Market Share

Brand $ Sales ÷ Total Category $ Sales — measures competitive position within the category

Baseline Sales

Expected sales without any promotional activity — represents underlying demand at everyday shelf price

Incremental Sales

Sales above baseline driven by promotional activity — the lift generated by a promotion event

Promo Lift %

Incremental ÷ Baseline — promotional efficiency · CPG benchmark is 15%+ for effective promotions

TPR

Temporary Price Reduction — shelf price reduced without feature ad or display · lowest ROI promo type

Feature

Brand in retailer weekly circular or digital ad — no price reduction required · drives awareness

Display

In-store secondary display — end-cap or aisle interrupt placement · no price change required

F&D

Feature & Display — combined circular ad + in-store display · highest lift at 44.9% in this category

YA

Year Ago — same period in the prior year · used for YoY performance comparison across all metrics

YoY

Year Over Year — % change vs same period prior year · primary growth metric in CPG reporting

L4W/L13W/L52W

Last 4 / 13 / 52 weeks rolling period — standard CPG reporting windows used by NielsenIQ & IRI

UPC

Universal Product Code — unique 12-digit barcode identifying each individual SKU at item level

SKU

Stock Keeping Unit — unique product variant defined by brand + flavor + pack size combination

Pack Size

Product weight variant — 150g · 200g · 250g · 300g in this category · key velocity differentiator

Sub-Category

Product grouping within Cookies — Sandwich · Chocolate Chip · Shortbread · Assorted Cookies

Category Sales

Total $ sales across all brands — denominator for Market Share and Fair Share Index calculations

Star Schema

Data model — one fact table (fact_sales) connected to 4 dim tables (product · retailer · date · promo)
